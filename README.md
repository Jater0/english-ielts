#### Frustrate

- vt. 使沮丧; 挫败; 阻止

例句: A large amount of work **frustrates** the workers.(大量工作使这些员工感到灰心)

> 记录日期: 2024.01.06

##### 派生词

**frustration**: n.沮丧, 不满

##### 单词变形

- 现在分词: frustrating

- 过去分词: frustrated

- 过去式: frustrated

- 第三人称单数: frustrates

##### 英文释义

v. To make somebody feel annoyed or impatient because they cannot do or achieve what they want.
